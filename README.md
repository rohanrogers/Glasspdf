# GlassPDF

Local-first document tools. Private by design.

PDF Canvas is now available — a visual workspace for managing and editing PDF pages, built entirely in the browser.

GlassPDF is a suite of professional document and image utilities that runs entirely within your web browser. Whether you are manipulating PDFs, previewing PSD files, or converting images, every operation is performed locally on your device. Your files are never uploaded, stored, or processed on an external server.

## Privacy

Your data belongs to you. GlassPDF guarantees zero data collection and zero server-side processing. Because all computations occur within the isolated environment of your browser, your files remain strictly on your machine. We cannot see, track, or access your documents, ensuring absolute confidentiality. All files are processed entirely in-memory and never leave your device.

## Features

- **Merge PDF**: Combine multiple documents seamlessly with drag-to-reorder.
- **Split PDF**: Extract page ranges into distinct files with live previews.
- **Compress PDF**: Reduce file size with structural re-bundling, zero pixel loss.
- **PDF Viewer**: Immersive local document viewer with fullscreen, zoom, and text search.
- **Security Studio**: Password-protect or unlock PDF documents with AES encryption.
- **PSD Studio**: High-fidelity Adobe Photoshop previews with a 4-stage rendering engine.
- **Image Converter**: Convert between PNG, JPG, WebP formats or combine images into PDF.
- **Image Compressor**: Optimize images with a real-time quality slider and live preview.
- **PDF Canvas**: Load multiple PDFs, see all pages at once, drag to reorder, rotate, delete, and export exactly what you need.

## Technology

GlassPDF leverages Next.js and modern browser APIs to deliver desktop-class performance on the web. By utilizing WebAssembly and advanced client-side rendering engines, heavy file processing that traditionally required backend servers is now executed instantly and securely on your own hardware.

## Open Source

GlassPDF is open-source software designed for the open web. It is built upon powerful open-source engines and community projects. All underlying components adhere to their respective open-source licensing.
